Team Endalex News

About: Team Endalex Esports

Team Endalex is a premier, world-class esport organization founded and based in London, England. Our professional teams and players compete in the most prestigious leagues worldwide. Gaming is our passion, we play to win.

Team Endalex has held divisions in numerous esports throughout its existence, establishing three by 2017, namely Counter Strike, Rocket League, and PUBG Battle Royale.

About: Our CEO

Alex Jakeman is the co-founder and CEO of esports organization Team Endalex. Prior to founding Team Endalex in 2015, Jakeman worked as the general manager and vice president of sales at Team Phase Up, one of the world's largest and most popular esports organizations. He has also worked at media website Endalex Media.
